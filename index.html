Tiger Transactions

Personal finance for Princeton students

The Problem

Finance, especially for college students, can often be a tough challenge to handle. Banking applications and websites are hard to read and even harder to understand. To Princeton students new to financial freedom, personal funds can easily slip out of control. Tailored specifically to people new to maintaining a budget, Tiger Transactions helps students manage finances with ease.

Benefits

Easily visualized transcations help plan a future budget.
Accessible to students with any experience in finance.
Free to use, always — a great tool for any college student.
