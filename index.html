Tiger Transactions

Personal finance for Princeton students

The Problem

Finance, especially for college students, can often be a tough challenge to handle. Current banking applications and websites use technical jargon and confusing designs that obscure the understanding of what personal finance is. To Princeton students with their first taste of financial freedom, personal funds can easily be regarded as unlimited. With a user-friendly interface tailored specifically to people new to maintaining a budget, Tiger Transactions helps students manage finances with ease.

Easily visualized transcations help plan a future budget.
Accessible to students with any experience in finance.
Free to use — great for any college student.
